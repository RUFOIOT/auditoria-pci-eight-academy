Executive Summary
Nicolás Ochoa is a serial education entrepreneur and international coach with 28 years of experience building and scaling education businesses in Latin America. He founded Corporación Jetmind — an ecosystem of 4 synergistic education units including Charlotte English School (Ecuador's #1 English school), Eight Academy (Ecuador's first metaverse K-12 school), Método Tomatis, and Método ILVEM. After leading the company through a critical pandemic recovery — growing revenue 4x from $3M to $12M — Nicolás is now positioning Jetmind for global expansion. His leadership style combines strategic vision, resilience, and a deep commitment to developing leaders, educators, and entrepreneurs across LATAM.
